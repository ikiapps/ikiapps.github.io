The number of years that it takes to create a sushi chef under traditional training is around 10. This extraordinary and refined art of bringing the sea to the table can be compared to the journey of making apps. Any discipline worth mastering requires this kind of investment and those that are truly passionate will tell you that it is only after such time that one is even ready to begin to learn.

Musings in humility aside, with 10+ years of programming experience and earning a BS in Physics and an MS in Computer Science, Daniel founded ikiApps for the purpose of helping to bring people’s ideas into reality through applications of software engineering. Perhaps an app or service is not tasty in the same way as fine sushi, but it can create a similar level of pleasure and satisfaction. Isn’t that wonderful?
